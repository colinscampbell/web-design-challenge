Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We looked at data from over 500 cities from the OpenWeatherMap API.

After assembling data, we used Matplotlib to plot cloudiness, humidity, wind speed, and maximum temperature for cities vs. their distance to the equator (latitude).
